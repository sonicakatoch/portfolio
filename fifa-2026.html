project

We were tasked to develop a creative direction for Toronto’s FIFA World Cup 2026 host city bid along with related collateral consisting of photography, sizzle videos, signage, decoration, gifting, a 50 page perfect bound book, and a 160 slide keynote to accompany speakers during their presentation.

OBJECTIVE

The City of Toronto and bid partners welcomed the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) delegation for the FIFA World Cup 2026 host city site visit. MLSE, Destination Toronto, Canada Soccer, Exhibition Place, the Province of Ontario and Government of Canada, businesses and community leaders met with the delegation to showcase Toronto’s diversity, capacity and collaboration. Notable Speakers included MLSE Chairman Larry Tanenbaum, Mayor John Tory, Pinball Clemens, and Dwayne De Rosario. As a Creative Studio, we aimed to showcase that hosting the world cup meant the world isn’t coming to Toronto, it’s already here. All in TO.
